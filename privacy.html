Privacy.

At Moorland View Care Home, your privacy is important to us. We publish below our privacy notice and cookies policy so that users of our website can see what we do to ensure privacy on digital platforms.

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Moorland View Care Home uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly, improve user experience, and provide information about how visitors use the site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure our website functions correctly and to help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can continue to improve it.

Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website.

This information may include:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the website
  • How visitors found the website
  • Device and browser information

This information helps us improve our website and the services we provide.

How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

Third-Party Content

Our website may contain embedded or linked content from third-party services, including Google Drive and YouTube.

When interacting with these services, they may place their own cookies on your device and collect information in accordance with their own privacy and cookie policies.

You can learn more by visiting:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Privacy Information

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control and manage cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies at any time, although doing so may affect the functionality of some parts of this website.
