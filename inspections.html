CQC Routine Inspection

March 2023

We were recently inspected by the CQC on 07-08 March 2023, in line with the routine inspections that occur for care homes in the UK. We have now been issued with the report, in which we have been rated as a good care home. We are delighted with this rating and would like to praise all colleagues for their hard work and dedication to Moorland View, our good rating is a testament to all their efforts.



BwD Quality Assurance Inspection

December 2022

The care home is delighted to announce that we were recently awarded a 70% quality assurance, being 1% away from being classed as an enhanced quality provider. We feel that our staff's tremendous service combined with changes in our services has allowed us to gain such a high rating. We hope you share our pride in these results. However, we are working towards being fully qualified as enhanced by the next inspection, by the end of the year.
